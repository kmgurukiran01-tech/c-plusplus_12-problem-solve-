# c-plusplus_12-problem-solve-
problem-statement:-
You are given a vector that contains pairs, each consisting of an integer and a string. Your task is to compute the total sum of all integers in these pairs, concatenate all the strings into a single string, and then print three values: the total sum, the concatenated string, and the length of this concatenated string.
​

Example 1
Input:
​
3
10 hello
20 world
30 welcome

Output:
​
60
helloworldwelcome
17

Explanation: The integers 10, 20, and 30 add up to 60, the strings "hello", "world", and "welcome" combine to "helloworldwelcome", and its length is 17.
​

Example 2
Input:
​
4
5 code
15 challenge
25 is
10 fun

Output:
​
55
codechallengeisfun
18

Explanation: The integers 5, 15, 25, and 10 sum to 55, the strings "code", "challenge", "is", and "fun" join to form "codechallengeisfun", and its length is 18.
